# Release Scope

## Included

- Android and iOS Flutter app.
- Level 1-400.
- generatorVersion 1 for Level 1-200.
- generatorVersion 2 for Level 201-400.
- Puzzle templates T01-T06.
- Clue rules C01-C10.
- Level 1-5 tutorial flow.
- New rule introduction flow for later rules.
- Local progress, tutorial, and feedback settings persistence.
- Resilient primary, pending, backup, and commit revision recovery.
- Sound effects and haptic feedback settings.
- Interstitial ads from the Level 6+ next-level flow.
- UMP consent flow and settings privacy options when required.
- Retry, restart, and next level flows.

## Excluded

- Level 401 and above.
- Rewarded ad hints.
- Banner ads.
- App open ads.
- Ad removal purchase.
- In-app purchase.
- Login.
- Server save.
- Cloud sync.
- BGM.
- Play time tracking.
- Personal best records.
- Level select screen.
- Daily puzzles.
- Skins, collections, currency, or inventory systems.

## Store Copy Guardrails

- Do not describe the game as infinite; the supported release range is Level 1-400.
- Do not mention rewarded hints, ad removal, cloud sync, login, or BGM.
- Do not claim that the app collects no data without store-policy review of Google Mobile Ads and UMP.
- Do not promise offline ad behavior; ad loading depends on network and SDK state, while puzzle play remains local.
